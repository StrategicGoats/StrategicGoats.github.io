Frequently Asked Questions

What is the inspiration around this game?

This game is inspired by the turn based strategy game 'Risk' with a few differences

How is this game different to Risk?

In Peril there are environmental hazards such as hurricanes and tsunamis which occur after each round of the game destroying a percentage of your troops in that specific area, giving the game an extra element to consider throughout game play

How do you win?

The game is won when one player owns all of the continents in the world map
