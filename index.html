Frequently Asked Questions

What is Peril?

Peril is a turn based strategy game inspired by the board game Risk. It combines the strategy of risk with new elements such as environmental hazards and blockades The game currently supports upto 2-4 player hotseat multiplayer and three Peril tailored maps. Players can even be AI controlled.

How does Peril differ from Risk?

Environmental hazards, such as hurricanes, add a element of unpredictability to game play and challenges give the player somthing to work towards. There is also a point economy in which the players can spend the points they accumulate on blockades and unit upgrades.
